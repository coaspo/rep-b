1. Near Infrared Camera (NIRCam)
   0.6 - 5 μm (HgCdTe; 37K)
   dichroic for 0.6-2.3 & 2.4-5
   earliest stars and galaxies
   young stars in the Milky
   Kuiper Belt objects
   coronagraphic imaging:
      characterize exo-panets
   10 HgCdTe arrays:
     8 0.6 - 2.5
     20.6 -5
   widefield grism (grating-prism) spectroscopy
   wavefront sensing for periodic alignment and phasing of the primary mirror
   9.7 arcmin^2  (jupiter is 0.7 arc min, moon/sun 30 arcmin)

2. Near InfraRed Spectrograph (NIRSpec)
   0.6 - 5.3 μm

   100 objects at 100 hour exposure
   microshutter array for aperture control
   2 HgCdTe detector arrays:
     0.6 - 2.5 μm / 0.6 - 5 μm

3. Mid-Infrared Instrument (MIRI)
   5 - 28 μm (Si:As 7K)
   redshift of  galaxies, new stars, comets, Kuiper Belt
   imaging with 9 broad-band filters 5.6 μm - 25.5 μm
   spectroscopy slitted/slitless
   3 Si:As detector arrays
   1 Lyot and 3 4-quadrant phase mask coronagraphs
   Cryocooler:

4. Fine Guidance Sensor /Near Infrared Imager and Slitless Spectrograph (FGS/NIRISS)
   0.8 - 5 μm (HgCdTe; 37K)
   3 detector arrays
