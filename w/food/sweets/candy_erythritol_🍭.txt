1   c erythritol (sugar alcohol, Truvia)
1/3 c water
    flavoring for 32 oz; for example crystal light lemonade

   Medium-high heat
   Stop stir when it boils
   At 290 F remove from heat
   Pour in 8 in baking pan
   Let cool 1 hour
   Break candy off the pan


Good for teeth & gums;
