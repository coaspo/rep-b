Fat carries flavor and adds texture

Butter inhibits protein development making tender and flaky pastry
Chose fat based on role:
  Flaky, creamy, light, flavor: main ingredient; butter olive oli...
  Crispy, heated: cooking medium; canola oil
  Tender: either role

Use unsalted butter and add salt as needed
Heat and brown butter to make nutty flavor
Remove butter solids to fry with clarified butter
